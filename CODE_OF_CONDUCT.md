# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

This project welcomes contributors who communicate clearly, respectfully, and constructively.

Expected behavior:

- discuss ideas with technical clarity
- critique changes, not people
- stay respectful across language and experience differences
- keep feedback actionable

Unacceptable behavior:

- harassment or personal attacks
- discriminatory language
- repeated bad-faith arguments
- spam, trolling, or deliberate disruption

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close discussions, or block participation when behavior violates this code.

## Reporting

If you encounter a serious conduct issue, open a private channel with the maintainer instead of escalating in public discussion threads.
